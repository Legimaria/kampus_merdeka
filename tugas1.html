Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology Universitas Sumatera Utara was established on September 6, 2011 marked by the issuance of the USU Chancellor's Decree No. 2360/UN5.1.R/SK/PRS/2011 concerning the establishment of the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology (abbreviated: Fasilkom-TI) Universitas Sumatera Utara


About Us

Program studi (Prodi) sarjana (S1) Ilmu Komputer (Computer Science) adalah salah satu program studi yang berada di dalam ruang lingkup disiplin Ilmu Komputasi (Computing Diciplines) sesuai dengan ACM Computing Curricula dan juga terdapat bidang ilmu lainnya seperti Teknik Komputer (Computer Engineering), Sistem Informasi (Information Systems), Rekayasa Perangkat Lunak (Software Engineering) dan Teknologi Informasi (Information Technology).


Purpose

No Karakteristik
1 Bersifat terbuka dan tanggap terhadap perkembangan teknologi informasi dan komunikasi (Information Comuniation Technology, ICT) maupun masalah yang dihadapi masyarakat, khususnya yang berkaitan dengan bidang komputer.
2 Memiliki ilmu serta keterampilan dalam bidang ilmu dan teknologi komputer yang dapat diimplementasikan pada kebutuhan masyarakat secara professional.
3 Menguasai kaidah ilmiah dalam berpikir, bersikap dan bertindak sebagai ilmuwan di bidang ilmu komputer.
4 Menyelenggarakan pendidikan tinggi bermutu untuk menghasilkan lulusan yang handal dan kompeten dalam bidang Intelligent Systems dan Information Retrieval.

Vision And Mission

Becoming an organizer of research-based higher education in the field of computer science/informatics and information technology that is adaptive, collaborative, and creative, as well as internationally qualified until 2022.

Develop the programs in Fasilkom-TI to be able to improve the education and research in the fields of Computer Science and Information Technology. Organize a number of groups in the field of expertise and research in computer science and information technology. Prepare graduates to be ready to compete with the global community in the fields of Computer Science and Information Technology research. Strive for the realization of Fasilkom-TI as a center for research, training and development of services in the fields of computer science and information technology. Develop innovative science and technology that can contribute to the better quality of people's lives in the framework of Tropical Science and Medicine,Agroindustry, Local Wisdom, Energy [sustainable], Natural Resources [biodiversity, forest, marine, mine, tourism], Technology [appropriate] and Arts [ethnic] (TALENTA) of USU. Organize Fasilkom-TI with a good governance in order to support the formation of an independent USU.
